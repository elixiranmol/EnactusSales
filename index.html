Dev Nagri is ours. Built in Roorkee by Uttarakhandi students, for the artisan communities of Kumaon and Garhwal. No middleman, no Trader-Sah, no Delhi showroom taking the cut. Every rupee walks back up the mountain to the hands that made it.

उनके हाथों से,
आपके घर तक।

From their hands, to your home.

Nine steps. Nothing hidden, nothing handed off to a chain of traders we cannot name. This is what "direct from the source" actually looks like.

०१ · 01

हमारे गाँव — Artisan Community

Crafted in homes across six districts of Devbhoomi

240+ artisans across 6 districts — Kumaon and Garhwal — craft in their own homes and workshops, on their own time.

०२ · 02

गाँव इकट्ठा — Village Collection

Gathered weekly at village collection points

Local collection points in each village gather finished work every week. No middleman collects on our behalf — we collect our own.

०३ · 03

जाँच — Quality Check

Inspected, and the maker's story recorded

Every piece inspected for quality at source. The artisan's name, craft process, and village are documented into the object's record.

०४ · 04

पैकिंग — Branding & Eco-Pack

'हर वस्तु की अपनी कहानी' — story-tag attached

Eco-friendly packaging, hand-tagged with the artisan's story card, origin tag, and craft certificate — all printed at the hub.

०५ · 05

यात्रा — Transportation

From the villages down to Roorkee

Carefully transported from the mountain villages to our coordination hub in Roorkee — packed by us, not by a contractor.

०६ · 06

रुड़की केंद्र — Roorkee Hub

Our central fulfillment, run by students

Our coordination hub at IIT Roorkee — inventory, final quality control, and order fulfillment, all run by Uttarakhandi students.

०७ · 07

बिक्री — Sales Channels

Our website, our Instagram, our exhibitions

Sold only through channels we operate — our website, our Instagram shop, and curated exhibition pop-ups we choose to be at.

०८ · 08

कूरियर — Courier Partner

Shipped via Delhivery, tracked end-to-end

Partnered with Delhivery for reliable pan-India delivery with full tracking from hub to doorstep.

०९ · 09

आप — You

A piece of Devbhoomi, at your door

Heritage arrives at your door — carrying the name of the hands that made it, and the village it came from.
